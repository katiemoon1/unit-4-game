Crystal Collector!

How to play:

At the start of the game, you will be given a number.

The four crystals displayed at the bottom of the page will add specific amounts of points to the initial value when clicked on.

You win if your score matches the random number (yay!) and lose if your number goes over.

At the start of each game, the values will be changed for each crystal.

Good luck!
